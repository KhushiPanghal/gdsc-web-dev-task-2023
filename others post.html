Information about NSUT

Netaji Subhas University of Technology, New Delhi (NSUT New Delhi ) is a recognised university by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Established in 1983, initially as Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology and affiliated to the University of Delhi, it became Netaji Subhas University of Technology in 2018. NSUT New Delhioffers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral courses in the field of engineering and technology. NSUT New Delhi provides an online admission procedure ensuring a smooth and convenient process.


Placements At NSUT

Netaji Subhas University of Technology, New Delhi (NSUT New Delhi ) has a well-functioning Training and Placement Cell which provides complete placement assistance to students. The Cell organises for pre-placement soft-skills and aptitude training and workshops, as well as arranges for career counselling and internship opportunities for the students. Campus recruitment drives are arranged and students get placed in reputed organisations such as Accenture, Adobe, Amazon, Infosys, Sapient, and many more.

Faculty

Netaji Subhas University of Technology, New Delhi (NSUT New Delhi) strives to provide excellent dedicated faculty in all departments as well as experienced technical assistants to enhance the learning experience of students. The faculty aims for an atmosphere that is academic, professional, confidential and safe for the students. Students repose faith in the faculty's academic splendour and take pride in their imposing eminence. No stone is left unturned by them to prepare the students for a brighter horizon.
